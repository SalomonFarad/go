About Us

Who We Are

Engineering Studio is a local collective of passionate engineers, designers, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation. We host workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects to turn ideas into reality.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to build a supportive environment where members can develop new skills, work on cutting-edge projects, and network with industry professionals.

What We Do

  • Workshops

    Hands-on workshops led by learners on topics like ,Youtube Automation, AI and more.

  • Collaborative Projects

    Team-based projects ranging from building prototypes to developing software solutions for real-world problems.

  • Networking Events

    Regular meetups and networking events to connect with local startups, and fellow innovators.

  • Hackathons

    Competitive hackathons that challenge our members to solve problems under time constraints, fostering creativity and teamwork.
